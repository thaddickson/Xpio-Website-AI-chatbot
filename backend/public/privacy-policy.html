Privacy Policy - AI Chat Assistant

Last Updated: January 21, 2025

Xpio Health ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our AI-powered chat assistant on our website.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP Address: Your device's IP address for security and fraud prevention
  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Referrer Information: The webpage that referred you to our site
  • Chat Metadata: Timestamps, conversation duration, and session IDs

Information You Provide:

  • Chat Messages: The content of your conversations with our AI assistant
  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, and organization details (when you choose to provide them)
  • Business Information: Information about your organization's needs, challenges, and interests in our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To respond to your inquiries and provide information about Xpio Health's services
  • Sales and Support: To connect you with our team members and follow up on your interests
  • Security: To prevent fraud, spam, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Analytics: To improve our AI assistant and understand how visitors use our chat service
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Xpio Health Team Members: To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our chat system (e.g., Anthropic for AI, Supabase for data storage, Slack for internal communications)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

Service Providers We Use:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) - AI chat processing
  • Supabase - Database and data storage
  • Railway - Hosting infrastructure
  • Slack - Internal team communications
  • SendGrid - Email notifications

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Location: Your data is stored on secure servers in the United States
  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our chat service is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access Controls: Only authorized Xpio Health team members can access conversation data
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information

5. Data Retention

  • Chat Conversations: We retain chat conversations for up to 90 days for quality assurance and follow-up purposes
  • Contact Information: When you provide contact information, we retain it until you request deletion or opt-out
  • IP Addresses: IP addresses are retained for 30 days for security purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications

California Residents (CCPA):

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

European Residents (GDPR):

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

7. HIPAA Notice

This chat service is NOT covered by HIPAA. Our AI chat assistant is for general inquiries about Xpio Health's technology services and is not intended for discussing protected health information (PHI) or patient-specific information. Do not share:

  • Personal health information
  • Patient records or data
  • Protected health information of any kind

If you need to discuss protected health information, please contact us through our HIPAA-compliant communication channels.

8. Children's Privacy

Our chat service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our chat service uses:

  • Session Storage: To maintain your conversation during your visit
  • Local Storage: To remember your conversation ID if you return
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the chat service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Third-Party Links

Our chat assistant may provide links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.
